What is Ursine?

Ursine is an advanced financial sentiment analysis tool built with Python. It leverages the power of the distilBERT model, meticulously fine-tuned on financial news data, to deliver precise sentiment insights. Whether analyzing market trends or dissecting corporate reports, Ursine offers a robust, machine-learning-driven solution to decode the sentiment behind financial information, enabling smarter, data-driven decisions.
